About GRAINSHARE

GRAINSHARE is a comprehensive platform dedicated to reducing food waste and improving food security by connecting surplus food donors with organizations that serve those in need. We create a transparent, efficient system that turns excess food into meaningful support for communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate food waste while strengthening community food security. Every year, billions of kilos of perfectly good food end up in landfills while millions go hungry. GrainShare bridges this gap by providing a seamless platform for food redistribution.

How It Works

GrainShare operates as a digital bridge between food donors and recipient organizations. Donors can easily list their surplus food, while organizations can request donations based on their needs. Our platform ensures safe, efficient coordination and tracking of food distribution.

User Roles

Our platform supports multiple user roles to ensure smooth operation and maximum impact:

  • Admin: Manages platform content, oversees user interactions, and ensures data accuracy.
  • Food Donor: Lists surplus food, coordinates donations, and tracks impact.
  • Recipient Organization: Requests food donations, manages logistics, and distributes to those in need.
  • Volunteer: Assists with food collection, distribution, and community outreach.
  • Data Analyst: Tracks food waste trends, analyzes data, and generates reports.
